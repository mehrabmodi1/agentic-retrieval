Margaret doesn't say anything for a long moment.

"I don't want to forget about her," Margaret finally says. "But I also don't want you to die because of her."

"I know."

"So what are you going to do?"

I don't have an answer to that. I'm a private investigator in a city where private information is for sale and public information is fiction. I'm a man with knowledge of a crime that nobody cares about because the person who committed it is too powerful to touch.

I'm exactly the kind of man that people like Sinclair count on to stay quiet.
